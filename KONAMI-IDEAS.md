# Konami Game Ideas (Future)

## Visual Polish
- Screen shake on big explosions / black hole detonation
- Ship afterimage trail when moving fast
- CRT scanline overlay for retro vibes

## Power-ups
- Homing missiles (pickup, 3 heat-seekers)
- Ricochet bullets (bounce off screen edges)
- Nuke (screen-clear panic button)
- Orbiting shield (stars circle you, block hits)

## Chaos Mode
- Boss letter — giant letter spawns, takes multiple hits, moves toward you
- Text fights back — some destroyed letters respawn and drift toward player
- Gravity mode — player becomes the black hole, text orbits you

## Meta Features
- Achievements ("First Blood", "Combo x10", "Pacifist")
- High score leaderboard (localStorage)
- Share button — screenshot final score
